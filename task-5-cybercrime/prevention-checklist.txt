Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in    India)


 Prevention Tips
*  Use strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
*  Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone
*  Enable 2-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps
*  Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages
*  Check website URL before entering personal details (look for https)
*  Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments
*  Keep your apps and phone updated regularly
*  Only download apps from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store


         


         UPI / Financial Safety (Important)
*   Never approve unknown UPI collect requests (Request money = scam    
* Do not scan random QR codes to receive money (you only scan to pay)






Reporting (Very Important)
*  Report cyber fraud immediately on National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
*  Call Helpline Number: 1930 for financial fraud help
